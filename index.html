Digital HV

Digital HV (pronounced Digital Hive) is an initiative to map the digital profiles of Hudson Valley businesses and technologists, and create meaningful connections between them. By supporting the development of mobile, web and embedded apps, we hope to grow awareness of local businesses to a global audience, and drive user engagement with them.
